OUR SERVICES

  • All corpse passes through a Pathologist specialist service.
  • Internal corpse is our priority.
  • The number of external corpse that can be admitted fully depends on the number of internal corpse.
  • We keep corpse till the relative, claim and take over the body for disposal.
  • All corpse with long duration without reclamation is reported to the council who grant the permission to bury.
  • Each corpse is placed into a single locker and it is being treated with formalin.
  • We can also take charge of the funeral ceremony and sympatize with bereaved family.
